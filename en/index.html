06 FAQ

What everyone asks

Does Neurocalc replace a radiologist or neurologist? +

No, and it doesn't try to. Neurocalc is an educational and study support tool. Every result carries an explicit medical disclaimer and should not be used as a definitive clinical report.

What kind of scans does the system accept? +

CT, MRI and other modality images, in slice format. It supports common image files exported from PACS/DICOM systems.

Is my data safe? +

Images are stored in AWS S3 with restricted access. Data is handled in accordance with Brazil's LGPD data protection law.

How does the credit system work? +

Each analysis uses credits based on the number of images uploaded. Before you confirm, the system shows the estimated cost and you decide whether to proceed. No surprises.

Is the Team plan more expensive per person? +

The Team plan is billed per seat (per active member). Credits renew monthly for each paid seat. The leader manages who joins, who leaves and the team's centralized balance.

Can I cancel or change plans? +

Yes. You can cancel automatic renewal at any time and go back to pay-as-you-go. Switching from pay-as-you-go to a monthly plan is done right from the Billing screen.
